What is a Pokedex ?

The Pokédex (ポケモン図鑑ずかん, Pokemon Zukan, Illustrated Pokémon Encyclopedia) is an electronic device created and designed to catalog and provide information regarding the various species of Pokémon featured in the Pokémon video game, anime and manga series. The name Pokédex is a neologism including "Pokémon" (which itself is a portmanteau of "pocket" and "monster") and "index".
